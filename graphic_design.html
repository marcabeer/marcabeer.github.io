Data visualization, illustration, and graphic design

Data visualization is a crucial component of science communication, particularly at the data / decision-making interface where individuals of varying backgrounds coalesce. I have over ten years of experience using graphic design software to generate high-quality illustrations, which have been featured in peer-reviewed publications, numerous presentations given by myself and colleagues, and grant proposals. Two of five grant proposals that I have contributed illustrations and conceptual diagrams to have been funded by the National Science Foundation. Below is a sample of my graphic design work.
